SafePal x MEXC GiftBox Exclusive

Mar 04,2025

Dear SafePal community,

SafePal is excited to announce the launch of a Giftbox campaign with crypto exchange MEXC, from 4th-14th March  9 AM UTC, 2025 with attractive USDT futures bonus.

To get started, download the latest version of the SafePal App here and visit the Questhub section to participate and learn more. 

Giftbox Tasks and Rewards:

There will be 3 forms of rewards for completing the following tasks:

Reward 1: 10 USDT Futures Bonus

Reward Pool: 20,000 USDT Futures Bonus
Slots: 2,000 (First Come, First Served)
Eligibility: Register in MEXC DApp in SafePal and net deposit at least 100 USDT & trade more than 5,000 USDT during the campaign

Requirements:

  • Register MEXC DApp within SafePal wallet
  • Accumulate a net deposit of 100 USDT in your account during the event period.
  • Trade at least 5,000 USDT in futures trading.

Reward 1 is limited to the first 2,000 participants.

Reward 2: 50,000 USDT Prize Pool

Reward Pool: 50,000 USDT Futures Bonus
Eligibility: Trade more than 30,000 USDT during the campaign

Requirements:

  • Trade at least 30,000 USDT during the event to qualify for rewards.
  • Rewards are distributed proportionally based on your trading volume compared to all qualifying participants.
  • Higher trading volumes earn larger bonus rewards.
  • Individual rewards are capped at 2% of the prize pool (maximum 1,000 USDT per user).

Reward 3: Trading Master Exclusive Rewards 

Reward Pool: 40,000 USDT Futures Bonus
Eligibility: Trading volume of 3 million USDT or more during the campaign

Requirements:

  • Reach a minimum trading volume of 3 million USDT during the event to qualify.
  • Rewards are distributed proportionally based on your trading volume relative to other qualifying participants.
  • Higher trading volumes earn larger rewards.
  • Individual rewards are capped at 2% of the prize pool (maximum 800 USDT per user).

Terms and Conditions

  1. The trading rewards will be distributed based on the participant's total valid Futures trading volume (opening amount + closing amount) and Spot trading volume (buy amount + sell amount) during the event. Only trades with fees greater than zero will be counted.
  2. All event rewards can be accumulated. Users who participate in multiple events will be eligible for higher total rewards.
  3. The rewards will be distributed by MEXC within 7-10 business days after the event ends. 
  4. Sub-accounts and institutional users are not eligible to participate in the event.
  5. In the case of bulk registration, malicious volume manipulation, cheating, illegal activities, fraud, or any other harmful behavior, MEXC will conduct a strict review and cancel the participation and reward eligibility.
  6. MEXC reserves the right to modify or change these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice, including, but not limited to, the cancelation, extension, termination or suspension of this event, the terms and criteria for eligibility.
  7. MEXC reserves the right of final interpretation of this event. If you have any questions, please contact our customer service team.
  8. Due to compliance regulations in various countries and regions, this event is not available to users Mainland China, United States (including all US territories), IR, Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, Cuba and Syria.

FAQs

1.Can users who have already signed up for a MEXC account with SafePal before the promotion participate?

This event is exclusively for new users who access the MEXC DApp via SafePal. If you already have a MEXC App account, you still need to register through MEXC DApp in SafePal wallet to join the campaign.

2.How to connect wallet to MEXC DApp?

Click ‘Wallets’ on menu bar – Choose the wallet you want to connect with – Click ‘Agree’

3.How to complete KYC verification on MEXC mini program?

Click ‘Wallets’ on menu bar – ‘Deposit’ – Follow the steps to finish KYC verification, E-mail and identity verification are needed to complete the process

4.How to trade on MEXC mini program?

Spot trading: 

Click ‘Trades’ on menu bar – Select the crypto you want to trade –  specify Buy/Sell, position size, and other relevant details – click ‘Buy’ or ‘Sell’ then confirm the transaction

Futures trading: 

Click ‘Futures’ on menu bar – Select the crypto you want to trade – specify the position size, leverage and other relevant details – click ‘Open Long’ or ‘Open short’ then confirm the transaction

5.How do I know if I am eligible for the rewards?

Your deposit and trading record will be reviewed by MEXC after the campaign. If it meets criterias, the rewards will be distributed to your MEXC account 7-10 business days after the campaign concludes. A notification in ‘Redeem Center’ will be sent to you once the reward is distributed. 

6.How do I check for the MEXC future bonus?

Please click ‘Wallets’ on the menu bar. Your future bonus will be shown in ‘Bonus’ under the ‘Futures’ tab.

7.How do I use the future bonus?

Rules for Using Bonus:

(1) The bonus can only be used for futures trading. While profits resulting from the trades using the bonus can be withdrawn, the bonus itself cannot be withdrawn.

(2) The bonus can be used as margin for trading, and can also be used for deduction of trading fees or funding fees, as well as to offset trading losses.

(3) Any asset transfers out of the futures account before the bonus is used up will result in the forfeiture of the bonus.

(4) Your bonus is valid for a maximum of 15 days. Any unused bonus will be automatically revoked 15 days after issuance. Please be aware of the potential liquidation risk resulting from this.

8.Can I get reward 1, 2 and 3?

Yes. The above reward 1, 2 and 3 can be accumulated if you meet the requirement of each.

9.Under what circumstances will the MEXC future bonus become invalid?

(1) Any asset transfers out of the futures account before the bonus is used up will result in the forfeiture of the bonus.
(2) Your bonus is valid for a maximum of 15 days. Any unused bonus will be automatically revoked 15 days after issuance. Please be aware of the potential liquidation risk resulting from this.
